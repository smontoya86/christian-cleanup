Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Information We Collect

Music Disciple collects minimal information to provide our biblical music analysis service:

  • Spotify Account Information: We access your Spotify playlists through read-only permissions to analyze song content.
  • Analysis Data: We temporarily process song lyrics and metadata for biblical analysis purposes.
  • Account Information: Basic profile information needed to maintain your account and preferences.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide biblical analysis of your music content
  • Maintain your account and analysis history
  • Improve our analysis accuracy and service quality

We never: Sell your data, share your listening habits, or use your information for advertising.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections and secure data storage. Your Spotify credentials are never stored on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Music Disciple integrates with Spotify's API under their terms of service. We do not share your personal information with any other third parties except as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Revoke Spotify access permissions at any time

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@musicdisciple.com.
